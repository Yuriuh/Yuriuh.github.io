US President Trump gave US Finance Minister Mukuchin 60 days (since March 22) to draft a plan to limit Chinese investment in the United States. 牛津经济研究院在发给第一财经记者的最新报告中称：“在我们看来，考虑到美国内对此的广泛支持态度，（美方）极有可能在中国投资方面出台更多限制。2017年，共和党和民主党参议员还联合推出了CFIUS审查扩容法案。”与此同时，有知情人士透露，此次特朗普政府还在权衡使用1977年《国际紧急经济权力法案》（International Emergency Economic Powers Act of 1977）（下称《紧急权力法案》）。这一法案赋予美国总统极大的权力，即在“不同寻常且巨大的危险”面前宣布国家处于紧急状态之下，并可以不受限制地征收关税。


The Oxford Economic Research Institute said in its latest report to the First Financial Reporter: “In our opinion, taking into account the widespread support attitude in the United States, (the US) is very likely to introduce more restrictions on Chinese investment. 2017 In 2009, Republicans and Democrats also jointly launched the CFIUS Review Expansion Act. At the same time, informed sources disclosed that the Trump administration is still weighing the use of the 1977 International Emergency Economic Power Act. This bill gives the U.S. president great power to declare the country in a state of emergency in the face of "unusual and immense dangers" and to impose tariffs without restrictions.
