About us

链创空间成立于2017年，致力协助优质项目的发展，并提供项目在不同阶段中，所需要的一切服务。总部位于武汉，在北京、上海、深圳、杭州、济南、泰国、新加坡、英国、澳洲设有分支机构和办事处。团队成员来自不同国家，深耕经营美国，澳洲，亚太，欧洲，东南亚等市场。

Founded in 2017, Blockchain Maker Space is committed to assisting in the development of quality projects and providing all the services needed for the project at different stages. Headquartered in Wuhan, it has branches and offices in Beijing, Shanghai, Shenzhen, Hangzhou, Jinan, Thailand, Singapore, the United Kingdom, and Australia. The team members are from different countries and are deeply pursuing markets in the United States, Australia, Asia Pacific, Europe, and Southeast Asia.
